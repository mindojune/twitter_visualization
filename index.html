#MeToo and #MAGA: A Tale of Two Hashtags

#MeToo and #MAGA are two hashtags at odds with each other that represent the opposite ends of the political spectrum in 2010s America. While #MeToo was very explicitly a Twitter-borne movement, #MAGA (acronym for #MakeAmericaGreatAgain) is a slogan of a presidentical campaign and resulting presidency very much driven by the Twitter horn.

This visualization tracks the history of each hashtag's popularity over the course of time, as well as seeing how other hashtags come into play in a particular time period specified by you.
